The Solar System

The Solar System is a group of celestial bodies including a star (the Sun) and the objects that orbit it — such as planets, moons, asteroids, and comets.

Main Planets

🌍 Earth

Our home planet. Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known planet to support life.

🪐 Saturn

Saturn is famous for its beautiful ring system. It is a gas giant and the second largest planet in our solar system.

☿ Mercury

Mercury is the smallest and closest planet to the Sun. It has no atmosphere and extreme temperatures.

🔴 Mars

Often called the "Red Planet", Mars has the largest volcano and dust storms in the solar system.

Interesting Fact

The largest planet in the Solar System is Jupiter, and the smallest is Mercury.

Conclusion

The Solar System is full of fascinating planets and mysteries. Continued space exploration helps us learn more every day!
